Affordable and Clean energy

There are 3 main contributers to air pollution

  • Mobile sources – such as cars, buses, planes, and trucks
  • Permitted sources – such as power plants, oil refineries, and factories.
  • Area sources – such as agricultural areas, cities, and wood burning fireplaces.

Easy ways to help counter the effects of air pollution

3 easy ways you can help air pollution

  • Conserve energy – remember to turn off lights, computers, and electric appliances when not in use
  • Be careful not to spill gasoline when filling up your car or gasoline powered lawn and garden equipment.
  • Choose environmentally friendly cleaners.

New types of clean energy can slow the pollution caused by coal factories

Solar panels are an effective source of clean energy

Wind turbines supply power to homes around the world

An average onshore wind turbine with a capacity of 2.5–3 MW can produce more than 6 million kWh in a year. Enough to supply 1,500 average EU households with electricity.
